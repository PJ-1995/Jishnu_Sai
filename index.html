About Me

I am P. Jishnu Sai, a cosmologist currently working in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore, India. My research interests lie in the study of the early universe, including the cosmic microwave background, dark energy, and large-scale structure. I hold a PhD in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science, where I was mentored by Dr. Rajeev Kumar Jain.
