About me

My name is Jishnu Sai Puthiyedath. I am a senior research fellow in the department of physics at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India. Currently in the final year of my Ph.D., working on early universe cosmology with Prof. Rajeev Kumar Jain.

My interests encompass a broad range of topics in theoretical physics, with a primary focus on early universe cosmology and the quantum aspects of gravity and gauge fields. I am particularly motivated by the interplay between theoretical models and cosmological observations, striving to bridge these domains effectively. But, I am generally interested in exploring any topic in physics that catches my curiosity!

My academic history, in reverse chronological order, is as follows (click here for my complete curriculum vitae):

  • Ph.D. in Physics, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India (2019 – Present)
  • M.Sc. in Physics, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India (2017 – 2019)
  • B.Sc. in Physics, Government College Madappally, Vadakara, Kozhikode, Kerala, India (2014 – 2017)
